## vs Feather (test_1) — ⭐ KEY DIVERGENCE
- **Different engine → different "top result."** Feather (script-pinned and agent) used **Google** and
  got **"SimplyCEO / Feather Browser"** (GitLab, an ANSI-C browser). C4C **chose DuckDuckGo** and got
  **"Feather Client"** (feathermc.com, a Minecraft launcher). Both are honest PASSes of *their* SERP —
  but the answers differ entirely because the agent picked a different engine.
- This is the cleanest demonstration in the whole comparison that **"the top result" is engine-relative**,
  and that a free agent's engine choice is itself a variable. C4C deliberately picked DDG to dodge bot
  detection; the Feather agent went straight to Google `/search?q=` and also got through.
- Both agents caught the namesake ambiguity and flagged that the true "browser" result was a runner-up.
