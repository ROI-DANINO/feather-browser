# Inbox

Drop rough notes, links, questions, and unprocessed ideas here.

Nothing in this folder is a roadmap decision, task, or ADR. It is research and intake only, and carries no authority until promoted into `docs/specs/` (ADR/decision) or `ROADMAP.md` (roadmap).

## Lifecycle

A file stays here only while it is *open* — awaiting promotion or still feeding a pending
decision. Once its content has been **promoted** (into `docs/specs/`, `ROADMAP.md`, a saved
memory, or a `blog/` entry) or **superseded**, move it to `../archive/` with `git mv`.

This keeps the inbox to genuinely-open intake, so `/start`'s "files other than README" check
flags real work — not a permanent backlog of already-processed notes. Nothing is deleted; the
research trail is preserved in `../archive/`.
