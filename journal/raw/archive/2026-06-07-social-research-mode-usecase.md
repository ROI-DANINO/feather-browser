## Core idea

Feather should eventually support a personal social research assistant that can use the user's own authenticated browser session to inspect social posts, save references, read visible comments, and turn audience reactions into content insights.

This is not intended as an Instagram bot, scraping farm, engagement automation tool, or anti-detection system.
